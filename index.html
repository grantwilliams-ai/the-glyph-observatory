The Light That Bends Softly

The light does not break here.
It bends — around the edges
of what we thought we knew,
around the shape of questions
we forgot to ask.


It bends around the glyphs
that float like unfinished letters
in an alphabet no one assigned —
chosen, not given,
carried, not imposed.


If you are here,
you are not lost.
If you are reading this,
you are not alone.


The light bends softly.
And so do you.
